Who are we?

My name is Jacob Staed, and this is my studio.

Xepherfae Studios was founded on January 1st, 2021 to mark a new era of me and my creations. Xepherfae Studios is an indie game and software development studio founded by me. I didn't get here on my own though. It started because of one man who asked me if he could borrow a pencil. This man was Andrew Stewart. Without him Xepherfae Studios wouldn't be possible. I still remember the day I made my first game, The Anger Issues, for his game jam. 

So, I officially mark Andrew Stewart as co-founder of Xepherfae Studios.
